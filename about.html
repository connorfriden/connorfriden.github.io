About Me

My name is Connor Friden, and I am an Interaction Designer based in Atlanta, Ga. I have learned to work as a team and how to be a meaningful contributor to a project. As a team member, I would consider myself a motivator and a creative influence. Working as a team can be difficult depending on the work that needs to be done, and I always try to keep morale up throughout the process. I try to stay positive no matter how daunting the task. I also thrive in a creative environment and I enjoy coming up with ideas and solutions. I am always willing to share my ideas with others without prioritizing my ideas over those of my fellow team members.
